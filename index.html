About RAISE 2025

RAISE 2025 condenses the essentials of modern robotics and AI into an intensive three-day boot camp that blends theory, hands-on practice, and a team challenge. This year's unifying theme is "AI-Empowered Robotics for Smart Agriculture."

Participants learn to design mobile robots that diagnose plant-health conditions with computer vision and Large-Language-Model (LLM) reasoning, then recommend ‚Äì or autonomously execute ‚Äì remedial actions.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of RAISE 2025, participants will be able to:

Deploy ROS 2 stacks for mobile manipulation and navigation
Implement YOLO-based vision pipelines tailored to agricultural crops
Integrate LLMs for multimodal (image + text) reasoning in ROS ecosystems
Prototype end-to-end agri-robot solutions within tight time-frames
Collaborate cross-disciplinarily under competition pressure
